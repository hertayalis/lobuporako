Jenna Raine - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as a member of the all girl group L2M; they released a debut single called "GIRLZ." The group has also found online success on musical.ly. She released her debut solo single "Us" in 2018, followed by two EPs called Nen and Be Like You in 2019. 

Before Fame

She enjoyed playing softball, dodgeball and volleyball competitively growing up.

Trivia

In 2018, she was an opening act for Max & Harvey on their UK/Ireland tour. She would join them on tour again in 2019. 

Family Life

She is from Westlake, Texas.

Associated With

She performed alongside Mariangeli Collado in L2M. She worked with Candice Pillay on her EP Nen.
