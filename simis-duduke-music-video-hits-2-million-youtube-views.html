Simis Duduke Music Video Hits 2 Million YouTube Views

Barely three weeks after it was uploaded, Nigerian singer Simi’s new single ‘Duduke‘ has crossed 2 million views on YouTube.

The pregnant wife of Adekunle Gold recorded the song in honour of her unborn baby. The song has been trending heavily with many using it for TikTok.

READ ALSO: Simi Explains Meaning Of ‘Duduke’

Simi, who could not keep calm about the new milestone of her track, took to Instagram to show gratitude.

‘Duduke’, which was released on 19 April 2020, currently sits top of iTunes Chart in Nigeria, and it’s Simi’s first official single for 2020.
