Rihanna Stuns in a Fringed Fendi Look for Date Night

If there's one thing we know about Rihanna, it's that she's always dressed for the occasion.

Late Sunday night, the singer was photographed leaving A-list Los Angeles restaurant Delilah with rumored boyfriend and rapper A$AP Rocky not far behind. Rihanna wore a vintage fringed velour mini layered under a matching shearling-trimmed jacket by Fendi, both of which come from fan-favorite Archive Vintage. Topping the look off with minimal accessories, she opted for a pair of all-black sunglasses by Italian eyewear brand Retrosuperfuture and barely-there sandals from her shoe collection with Amina Muaddi from her now defunct Fenty fashion line . A messy-chic top knot completes the killer date night ensemble, lending it just the right casual balance.

Known for being equally as stylish, A$AP wore a leather trucker jacket with a graphic tee, logo-embossed pants from cult brand Marine Serre, and round-top combat boots to hit up the party. Rocky, who's real name is Rakim Mayers, trailed slightly behind.

A notoriously fashionable pairing, the two are also longtime friends who have dated on and off since meeting back in 2012. It wasn't until November of last year that the ultra-chic duo were back on again, almost a year after Rihanna had called it quits with Saudi businessman Hassan Jameel. Since rekindling their relationship, the couple have been seen out together both in New York and Los Angeles, churning out a slew of photo-worthy looks along the way that the paps are more than happy to document.
