Unfortunate placement of strip club billboard seems to answer question posed by billboard in front

An unfortunate placement of two billboards in Melbourne had locals in stitches on Monday.

The first advertisement by Work Safe shows a mother and a her young daughter asking: 'Where's daddy?'

A second billboard, directly behind, broadcasting the services of Melbourne's Spearmint Rhino, a notorious strip club.      

A Reddit user has posted an image of coincidental billboard placement advertising two very different things but seem to compliment each other in a strange way

Reddit user u/5_Frog_Margin posted the image from Melbourne to the site on Sunday, with the caption 'Really bad billboard placement'. 

Work Safe, which promotes safe working environments, probably didn't realise where their ad would appear on the busy stretch of road.
