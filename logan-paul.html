Who is Logan Paul?

Logan Paul was born in Westlake, Ohio on April 1, 1995. Jake Paul, his younger brother, is also a social media sensation. Logan began making YouTube videos when he was 10 years old, through a channel he created called Zoosh.

In 2012, he was an All-Star linebacker for the Westlake High School football team. In high school, he was also a state champion wrestler, and he went on to study engineering at Ohio University. Between 2013 and 2014, he increased his social media following from 900 to 1.5 million. He was particularly well-liked on the social media app Vine. He dropped out of Ohio University in 2014 to relocate to Los Angeles.
