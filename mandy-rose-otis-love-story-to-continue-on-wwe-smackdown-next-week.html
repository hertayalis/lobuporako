Mandy Rose &amp; Otis Love Story To Continue On WWE SmackDown Next Week

Mandy Rose and Otis have been off television for a few weeks. It’s unclear why they were out of action. Next week they will pick up where WWE left things.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE Friday Night SmackDown from this week.

During WWE Friday Night SmackDown this week, they advertised that Otis and Mandy Rose will be on the show next week. They also played a highlight package showing their love story to update fans.

It wasn’t clear what Otis and Mandy Rose will be doing next week, but their romance will continue. Otis is still Mr. Money In The Bank, so he has that going for him too.
