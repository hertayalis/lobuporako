MUSIC: Tink Chasin

Sensational American rapper, Trinity Laure’Ale Home, known within the showbiz circles as Tink comes through with a new song titled; ‘Chasin’ off her just released studio album, “Heat Of The Moment”

The latest body of works comes in a package of fourteen dope tracks with features from Highy rated Nigerian singer, Davido alongside fellow American acts namely, Kodak Black, Jeremih and Yung Bleu.

Tink is well known for her track, ‘Treat Me Like Somebody’ and also fro appearing on Jerimih’s song, ‘Don’t Tell Nobody’. She has dropped seven mix-tapes since 2012.

Tink has also been an independent artiste due to labels refusal to release her debut album. Her 2014 mixtape, “Winter’s Diary 2: Forever Yours” made it to the top 10 R&B album in both Rolling Stone and Billboard magazines.
