Chris Pine bio, education, career, net worth, relationship

Who is Chris Pine?

Chris Pine is an actor from the United States. His most well-known performances include James T. Kirk in the Star Trek film reboot series, Steve Trevor in the Wonder Woman and Wonder Woman 1984 movies from the DC Extended Universe, Will Colson in Unstoppable, and Toby Howard in Hell or High Water.

Chris Pine bio

Pine was born in Los Angeles, California, on August 26, 1980. His mother, Gwynne Gilford, is a former actress who went on to become a psychotherapist, and his father, actor Robert Pine, co-starred as Sergeant Joseph Getraer on CHiPs.

Chris Pine sibling

Katherine, his older sister, is an actress as well.

Chris Pine education

After finishing high school at the Oakwood School, Pine went on to get a Bachelor of Arts in English from the University of California, Berkeley in 2002.

As a member of the UC Berkeley Theater Department, Pine appeared in Orestes and Shakespeare at Zellerbach Hall as well as a Caryl Churchill piece at La Val’s Subterranean Theater.

He spent a year as an exchange student at the University of Leeds in the UK.

Following his graduation from Berkeley, he studied at the American Conservatory Theater in San Francisco and attended the Williamstown Theatre Festival.

Chris Pine career

Chris began acting in short films in 2003 after leaving the theater scene and began applying for TV roles. He was eventually cast in small roles on shows including ER, The Guardian, and CSI: Miami.

He gained worldwide acclaim for his portrayal in the play “Fat Pig” in 2007, when he appeared as the primary character’s best friend and was described as a “alpha male.”

In 2017, Pine was spotted performing the vocals for “SuperMansion’s” second season. He also ended up narrating “Breakthrough” on the National Geographic Channel.

The most significant role of his whole career arrived in the middle of 2017 when he played Steve Trevor, a World War II British spy and Gal Gadot’s love interest, in his most commercially successful movie to date, “Wonder Woman.”

Chris Pine net worth

Chris Pine’s net worth is projected to be $35 million as of December 2022.

Chris Pine relationship

From early 2022 till the end of 2018, Pine was dating British actress Annabelle Wallis. From late 2011 until early 2013, he was dating South African model Dominique Piek.
