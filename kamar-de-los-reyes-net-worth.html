Kamar de los Reyes Net Worth: How Much Is Kamar de los Reyes Worth?

Kamar de los Reyes Net Worth: How Much Is Kamar de los Reyes Worth?: Puerto Rican actor, Kamar de los Reyes, best known for playing a gang member-turned-cop in the soap “One Life to Live” and a villain in the video game “Call of Duty: Black Ops II,” has passed on.

What Is Kamar de los Reyes Net Worth?

Before Kamar de los Reyes died in December 2023, he had an estimated net worth of $8 million. He accumulated such fortune from his career as a television, movie, and voice actor

According to Lisa Goldberg, his wife, actress Sherri Saum’s publicist, the television, movie, and voice actor, Kamar de los Reyes died in Los Angeles at 56 on Sunday, December 24, 2023, following a brief battle with cancer.

At the time of his death, de los Reyes was filming the CW sports drama “All American” and had recently shot roles in the forthcoming series “Daredevil: Born Again” and the miniseries “Washington Black,” according to Lisa Goldberg

De los Reyes was born in Puerto Rico and raised in Las Vegas. He caught the bug for acting when he arrived in Los Angeles in the late ’80s.

In “One Life to Live”, de los Reyes starred as Antonio Vega, a former gang member who became a lawyer and then a cop. In “Call of Duty: Black Ops II,” he played the villain Raul Menendez.

His film credits include; Ghetto Blaster, Coldfire, Lethal Ninja, The Silencer, Street Knight, Fatherhood, Daedalus Is Dead, The Cell, Cayo, Hot Guys with Guns, LA Apocalypse, and Kingdom.

De los Reyes also appeared in the films, Shooter, Sleepy Hollow, MacGyver, The Gifted, SEAL Team, The Passage, The Mentalist, Reed Between the Lines, The Rookie, All American, and The Mentalist.

According to a statement by the family, “De los Reyes lived in Los Angeles, however, his heart never left Puerto Rico,”. It added that the actor had been active in the recovery efforts after Hurricane Maria in 2017.
