Hailey Bieber Is Reviving Holiday Glamour in a Red Fur Coat

Date-night style just received an extra dose of glamour, thanks to Hailey Bieber’s extravagant take on festive dressing.

On Wednesday, Bieber brought the heat with her vibrant look for her company Rhode’s holiday party in Los Angeles. Teaming up with celebrity stylist Dani Michelle, the model pulled pieces from Ferragamo’s Fall/Winter 2023 collection.

This included an incredible laminated jersey minidress that evoked ’80s holiday party style with its metallic maroon sheen, ruched gathering details, and bodycon silhouette. Outside of the venue, she stayed warm by layering with a long shearling coat in a dramatic bright red shade. (The outerwear, which also comes in a cloudy gray, has an eye-watering price tag of $9,800.)

To complete the party outfit, Bieber also wore her go-to sheer tights from Calzedonia, black leather pumps, narrow black rectangular shades, and sculptural gold earrings.

Justin Bieber accompanied his wife to the celebration, dressing to the nines in a relaxed black baggy suit with a white button-down, a beige baseball cap worn backward, and chunky black loafers.

BACKGRID

This isn’t the first time Hailey has shown off her holiday wardrobe this season.

In mid-November, she posted photos to Instagram from her 27th birthday party. She kicked off the milestone in a vintage Thierry Mugler bodycon dress. The strapless piece was made out of luscious ruby-red velvet fabric, and featured a unique angular neckline. She accessorized with gold accents, including twisted gold hoop earrings, a gold watch, and an array of delicate gold rings.
