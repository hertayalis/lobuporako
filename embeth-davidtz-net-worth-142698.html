Embeth Davidtz Net Worth

Embeth Davidtz is an actress best known for her roles in Schindler's List, Matilda, and The Amazing Spider-Man. She was born on August 11, 1965 in Lafayette, IN and raised in South Africa. She played Jennifer Honey in the children's film Matilda and Mary Parker in the 2012 film The Amazing Spider-Man.
