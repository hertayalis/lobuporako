Moose Wins TNA World Title At Hard To Kill

TNA Wrestling brought Hard to Kill for fans this week, and it was quite a show. In the end, the main event match saw a huge title change as the company starts their path down a new road.

Alex Shelley defended his brand-new TNA World Title against Moose in the main event of Hard to Kill. In the end, there was yet another title change to close out the event.

Moose is now the TNA World Champion as the company embarks into a new era as a rebranded entity. He has always been loyal to TNA as well, because he’s had other offer in the past.

We will have to see what is next for Moose as TNA World Champion. He got off to a good start at Hard to Kill. After Moose won the title, former WWE Superstar Dolph Ziggler showed up to end the event with big surprise.

Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play coverage of tonight’s show over in our TNA Wrestling coverage hub. It promises to be a memorable event for many reasons, so we’ll have to wait and see how the show will turn out.
