Kylie Jenner Dyes Her Hair Chocolate Brown

Quarantine hasn't stopped Kylie Jenner from experimenting with different hairstyles.

After dyeing her hair golden blonde in February, the star has been platinum blonde and tried out cotton candy pink, the unofficial quarantine celebrity hair trend.

All of Jenner's looks from the past couple months can be credited to her vast wig closet, but now, she's decided to make a more permanent hair change.

Jenner is now officially a brunette. She debuted her new hair color on Instagram with a photo of her trying on a corset. Her rich chocolate brown hair was styled in glam Old Hollywood-inspired waves.

Celebrity hairstylist Chris Appleton (he's Kim Kardashian West's go-to guy) confirmed that Jenner's new look is the real deal and not a wig.

"Colored Kylie's hair light chocolate brown," he captioned his repost of Jenner's photo. "Who's into it?"

And it seems like the stylist's followers are loving Jenner's rich chocolate brown hair.

"Loves it," Paris Hilton wrote accompanied by the fire emoji.

"I'm in love!" commented another fan.

Other followers asked Appleton for the extract formula he used on Jenner and tips for keeping a color like hers from getting too warm and brassy, a common problem for lighter shades of brunette hair.

"I lighten the hair first and then use neutral and cool tones," he told one follower.

Jenner's new chocolate brown hair color busts the myth that you should only go lighter for summer, but given her track record, it's only a matter of time before she gets bored and switches things up again.
