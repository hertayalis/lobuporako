Éder Militão is one of the finest defenders in the Spanish La Liga right now. Since he joined Real Madrid in 2019-2020, he has been the center of attention for making crucial and critical tackles.

Éder Militão Biography

Éder Gabriel Militão is a professional footballer who plays as a center-back for the Brazilian National Team and his club, Real Madrid.

Militão started his career in São Paulo. He joined the Brazilian Série A club in 2017 and managed to score 4 goals in his 57 appearances for São Paulo before his departure.

He joined the Primeira Liga club, Porto, for a season but his performance was very impressive that it caught the eyes of the elite club, Real Madrid.

Militão joined Real Madrid in 2019-2020 and has been with the La Liga giants since then making 108 appearances and scored 7 goals so far.

How old is Éder Militão?

Éder Militão was born on 18th January 1998 in Sertãozinho São Paulo which is in Brazil. Militão is currently 24 years old.

What position does Éder Militão play?

Éder Militão plays as a center-back and serves as a wall for the Brazilian National Team’s defense and also for Real Madrid.

Éder Militão career

The 24-year-old defender has made a very good mark since joining Real Madrid. He has made a total of 212 appearances and scored 16 goals throughout his career.

He had his first International call up in 2018. Militão has since then made a total of 26 appearances for Brazil and scored only a goal.

He was part of the Brazilian National Team that won the Copa América in 2019 and also the Superclásico de las Américas in 2018.

Éder Militão has also won the La Liga title twice, Supercopa de España 2 times, the UEFA Champions League, and also the UEFA Super Cup in 2020.
