Biography

Bob Huggins was born in Morgantown, WV on September 21, 1953. College basketball coach nicknamed “Huggy Bear” who began coaching at West Virginia University in 2007 and won a Big East Championship in 2012. He took the job after spending seventeen successful years as the coach of the Cincinnati Bearcats. He succeeded John Beilein as the head coach at West Virginia University in 2007. He attended Indian Valley South High School in Ohio, where he led the basketball team to a 26-0 record as a senior.
