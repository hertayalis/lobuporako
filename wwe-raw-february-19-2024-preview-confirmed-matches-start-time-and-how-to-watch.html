WWE RAW February 19, 2024 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How to Watch

WWE is preparing for a live episode of RAW on February 19, 2024, with the intention of making it a standout event. Fans can anticipate an exciting show filled with compelling content from beginning to end.

Tonight’s episode of WWE RAW will be broadcast live from the Honda Center in Anaheim, California. The show will air at 8 PM ET on the USA Network and is the go-home edition of Monday Night RAW ahead of the Elimination Chamber Premium Live Event on February 24th.

This week’s show features GUNTHER defending the Intercontinental Championship against Jey Uso, a “Last Chance” battle royal which will decide the last entrant in the women’s Elimination Chamber match.

DIY & Awesome Truth will unite to square off against The Judgment Day in an eight-man tag team match, and Cody Rhodes will take on Drew McIntyre in a singles match. Chad Gable will also square off against Ivar in one-on-one action.

WWE RAW Card for February 19, 2024 Episode:

  • Cody Rhodes vs. Drew McIntyre
  • Chad Gable vs. Ivar
  • WWE Intercontinental Championship: Gunther (c) vs. Jey Uso
  • DIY (Tommaso Ciampa & Johnny Gargano), The Miz & R-Truth vs. The Judgment Day (Damian Priest, Finn Balor, Dominik Mysterio & JD McDonagh)
  • Last Chance Battle Royal To Qualify For Women’s Elimination Chamber: Shayna Baszler vs. Michin vs. Zoey Stark vs. Alba Fyre vs. Zelina Vega
