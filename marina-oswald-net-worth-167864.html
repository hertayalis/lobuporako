Marina Oswald Net Worth

Marina Oswald was born on July 17, 1941 in Russia and is best known as the widow of Lee Harvey Oswald, the man convicted of shooting President Kennedy. She was married to Oswald from 1961 until his death in 1963. Marina has since become a public figure, appearing in documentaries and interviews to discuss her life with Oswald and her thoughts on his involvement in the assassination.
